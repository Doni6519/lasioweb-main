Cooking Tips

  1. Proper Knife Skills

    2. Learn basic knife cuts like julienne,dice, and chop to speed up your prep time and improve the presentation of your dishes.Always keep your knife sharp for safer and more efficient cutting.

  2. Seasoning At The Right Time

    3. Add salt early when cooking soups or stews, but for more delicate dishes like salads, season right before serving to enhance flavours without over salting.

  3. Use Fresh Herbs

    4. When using fresh herbs like basil or parsley, add them toward the end of cooking to preserve their vibrant flavor. Dried herds are better are better for longer cooking dishes.

  4. Let Meat Rest

    5. After cooking, let meat rest for a few minutes before slicing.This allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring a moist and flavorful bite.
